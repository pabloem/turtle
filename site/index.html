Think in a New Way

The Korean language is very rich, and its vocabulary is hard to learn. The Hanja Explorer is an innovative tool that helps you study Korean vocabulary by visualizing the meaning and the similarities of different words.

What is Hanja?

Korean language has evolved over time; borrowing words from different languages. Hanja means 'chinese character', and about 70% of Korean vocabulary uses Hanja in some form. Something that's great about Hanja is that they can be mixed and matched to create new words.

Why study Hanja?

Traditional education of Korean as a foreign language has largely ignored the role of Hanja in the study of Korean vocabulary. On the other hand, materials that are specific for the study of hanja place too high a value in learning how to write a character, making the process slow and difficult. These practices are not useful, nor practical.

Here, you'll learn what are the building blocks of words; and you will be able to use them when writing and reading Korean right away.

Study anywhere

The Hanja Explorer will work perfectly on your computer, your tablet, or your phone; so that you can study wherever and however you want.
